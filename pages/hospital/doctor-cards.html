The department of neurology at our consists of a team of highly competent neurologists available round the clock, equipped with state-of-the-art technology and equipment providing world-class patient care with a human touch. The department of neurology is the centre of excellence and is a tertiary care referral centre with patients not only coming from all over the states of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, rest of India and also out of the country.
